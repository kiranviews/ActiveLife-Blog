This Week On Leisure

Cruise to nowhere

Travel bans across the various countries have prevented people from going on their holiday trips. It has rather been a very mentally taxing period in our lives due to this pandemic and we all want an avenue to embark on a mini holiday getaway to take some time off and to rejuvenate ourselves. So, cruise companies have started on this new concept called, Cruise To Nowhere where passengers can spend some days on a cruise and enjoy their time with their loved ones. The cruise won't dock in any specific location. Hence, the holiday goers can use the various amenities on the cruise and treat it as a fancier version of a staycation. To prevent any health violations, cruises have intensified all the necessary health checks and sanitation processes. So, would you try this whole new variant of a holiday getaway?

Art of Baking

When lockdown happened, many started picking up new hobbies to keep themselves occupied. One such popular hobby was baking. Baking is not only a hobby but also a skill. It does have a steep learning curve as it requires many techniques to get the outcome right. Like many other skills, with practice, baking can be a really good skill to add to your library. Making sourdough bread became a huge trend. However, it is not as simple as making a cupcake. It requires making a starter which needs to be fermented for some time. Followed by delicate kneading and patience to let the dough proof, it takes many tries and immense determination to master the skill of making sourdough. You may ask, Why go through that much trouble? Well, nothing beats home cooking. Learning how to bake allows us to make freshly made breads at home and it also gives us a sense of satisfaction and pride. Nothing beats the feeling of baking something nice for our loved ones. So, would you pick up baking as a new hobby?
