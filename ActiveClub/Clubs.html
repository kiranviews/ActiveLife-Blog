Chess is Epic

Are you those who think playing chess is only associated with nerds? Well, let us change your view on chess. Chess is one of the longest played strategy games which requires a high level of skills. This beautiful game is played both in a casual setting or highly intense competitions. Chess is not only an amazing hobby, it also has positive impacts on you. Research has proved that playing chess boosts your memory and attention span. Playing chess and working on it also boosts your strategic thinking abilities. Chess can be quite complex to start learning but as you start playing more games, you will get more fluent. Hope you are inspired to pick up this game now. Give it a try, you might be the next chess master!

Our education system has cultivated this habit in us since young but as we grow older, with work and other commitments, we find it hard to put aside some time to read. Learn to Code

The Digital Era is here and it's going to revolutionise the way the world is going to function. In a few years time, your job might be at the risk of being replaced by a robot or being digitised. Are you ready to face this digital transformation? The time is here for everyone to start learning how to code. Coding is not only for software engineers and data analysts. It's an important skill set that is extremely important in the current working world. Learning how to code will help you understand the new technologies in a deeper way and will allow you to keep yourself updated. You can learn how to digitise your ideas and businesses which will help you in the future and even during tough times like a pandemic. So, we highly encourage you to start learning how to code. There are many online tutorials like Codecademy and Udemy that will give you step-by-step guidance on learning a programming language. So, our recommendation is to start learning Python. It's a relatively simple yet powerful language that is commonly used in many big software companies like Google.
